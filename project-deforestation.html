Deforestation Detection in Peru

Student Researcher 2019 - 2020 UTEC, Lima

Overview

In this project, I developed satellite image processing pipelines to quantify deforestation in the Peruvian Amazon using Sentinel and Landsat data. I engineered vegetation indices and change-detection methods to estimate deforested areas, achieving approximately 5% error.

The work resulted in a peer-reviewed publication indexed as a book chapter in the Proceedings of the 6th Brazilian Technology Symposium.

Key Contributions

  • Processed multispectral satellite imagery at regional scale
  • Designed vegetation indices and change-detection algorithms
  • Quantified deforestation with validated accuracy
  • Published results in an international conference proceedings
